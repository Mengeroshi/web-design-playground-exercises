Typography

A Glosary of Terms
ascender
The part of tall lowercase letter such as b or h that extends above lowercase letters such as a and c
baseline
The invisble line upon which lowercase characters such as o and w appear to sit.
descender
The part of lowercase letter such as g or y that extends below the baseline
leadinrg
(pronnunced ledding) See line height
line height
The distance between the baselines of two adjacents lines of text
x height
The height of typeface's lowercase x
